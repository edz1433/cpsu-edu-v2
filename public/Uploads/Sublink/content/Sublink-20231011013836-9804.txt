<p><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;">March 30 &amp; 31, 2023 - The turn-over and installation of Starbooks, and forum on food safety and the ideal and practical “Pinggang Pinoy” at Tagukon National High School, and Camingawan National High School, respectively were realized through the Department of Science and Technology (DOST) – Region VI in partnership with CPSU-Research and Development Center.</span></p><p><img src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/DOST, CPSU conduct turn-over rites on Starbooks.jpg" style="width: 945px;"><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><br></span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;">Starbook is a stand-alone information source designed to reach-out those with limited access to S&amp;T information resources. The project with its benefits as discussed by the DOST coordinator, Sir Russel Renz Garcia, aims to provide Science, Technology, and Innovation-based content in various formats to students and other constituents in geographically-isolated and economically-challenged schools and communities throughout the country. Starbooks software contains hundreds of thousands of digitized science and technology resources in various formats (text and video/audio) placed in specially designed “pods” set in a user-friendly interface.</p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;">Meanwhile, the Food Safety and “Pinggang Pinoy” as discussed by DOST VI director, Engr. Allan Francis Dara-ug, aims to educate and urge every Filipino family to provide a balanced meal even with their usual food available at home. He particularly raised the awareness of participants on the proper handling of foods.</p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><img src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/DOST, CPSU conduct turn-over rites on Starbooks-1.jpg" style="width: 945px;"></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;">The Tagukon and Camingawan faculty, staff, students and parents expressed their gratefulness to the team for the opportunity to be chosen as recipients to receive the Starbooks. With it, students could now take advantage of the provided tools that are very beneficial to their education.</p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;">The Personnel who joined the Tagukon National Highschool turn- over were Mrs. Alona T.
