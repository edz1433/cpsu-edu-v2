Camingawan, Kabankalan City.</span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><img alt="" src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/fabric-1.jpg" style="border-width: initial; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">A total of 35 Camingawan -Tagukon Farmers’ Association or CATAFA members were taught on the principles of machine fabrication and were given hands-on training in making basic farm tools.</span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">Dr. Aladino Moraca, CPSU president, said that this activity will provide huge strides to the farmers to better meet their needs by raising the productivity and competitiveness of the country’s agriculture sector.</span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">Instrumental to this realization is the RU Foundry and Machine Shop Corporation (RUFMSC) headed by its owner and CEO Sir Ramon Uy, Sr. who donated several machines and equipment for the fabrication of various farm tools. The initiative was part of the company’s farmers’ assistance program.</span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">“This is now the living testimony to the agreement made by CPSU to the CATAFA, RUFMSC and Eco Agri Foundation last February 10, 2021 on the establishment of a small scale “pandayan” as a start up support facility and equipment for community development project,” Dr. Moraca said.</span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">Farm tools manufactured from the said workshop were bolo, crowbar, hoe, grab-hoe, axe and many more.</span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">Speakers during the said training were Engr. Their team was headed by Engr.
