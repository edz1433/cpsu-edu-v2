Must pass the oral interview and admission test</p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;">4. For Ph.D. applicants with non- thesal Master’s Degree, the following are the requirements:</p><ol style="margin-bottom: 10px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><li style="">He/She must enroll residency courses of 12 units (3 core courses, 1 major course) for two (2) semesters of the Master’s Program equivalent to the PhD/doctorate program intended.&nbsp;</li><li style="">Out of the 12 units to be enrolled, 9 units will be taken for the First Semester covering subject loads of 2 core courses and 1 major course.</li><li style="">While during the Second Semester, a 3-unit core course on Methods of Research will be enrolled.&nbsp; At this time, he/she is expected to have an output of a research proposal at the end of the semester and must pass the pre-oral defense.</li><li style="">For his/her final semester, enrolment on Thesis Writing (6 units) is needed for the research conduct and must pass thesis final defense.</li><li style="">He/she is required to comply and submit the hardbound copies of his/her master’s thesis before admission to the PhD/doctorate program.</li></ol>
