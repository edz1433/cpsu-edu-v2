<p>CPSU Rondalla in their premier performance during the monthly convocation held last May 3, 2023 &ldquo;wowed&rdquo; the audience in their rendition of &ldquo;The Prayer&rdquo; and &quot;Lupang Hinirang.&quot;</p>

<p>The team, organized and trained by Mr. Francis Emoy, COTED faculty, under the Kinetics, Sports and Cultural Department (KSCD) of CPSU, was among the key performers playing the bandurria instrument.&nbsp;</p>

<p>More of their performances will be witnessed in the upcoming events of the University.</p>

<p>KSCD Director, Rachel Robles, thanks the support of the administration for the realization of this long-time dream for the cultural group.</p>
