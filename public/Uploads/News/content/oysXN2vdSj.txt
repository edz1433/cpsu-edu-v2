<p>Central Philippines State University Moises Padilla Campus&nbsp;has finally become one of the&nbsp;MikroTik Academies&nbsp;in the Philippines. Being part of the MikroTik Academy allows CPSU Moises Padilla Campus to offer MikroTik Certified Network Associate <strong>(MTCNA)</strong> course for free to university students.</p>

<p><img alt="" src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/69457827_3003681489674209_552055914903896064_n.jpg" style="height:300px; width:400px" />&nbsp;<img alt="" src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/70183651_383220639240677_7168842394652114944_n.jpg" style="height:300px; width:400px" /></p>

<p>Merick A. Paculanang,&nbsp;I.T. Instructor&nbsp;of the College of Computer Studies, CPSU Moises Padilla Campus attended the MikroTik academy trainer&rsquo;s boot camp and certifications exam sponsored by Philippine Society of Information Technology Educators (PSITE-6) Inc. on July 1 &ndash; July 6, 2019 at Days Hotel, The Atrium, Iloilo City, Philippines. The training and exam qualified <strong>Mr. Merick Paculanang </strong>to become a MikroTik Certified Academy Trainer. He also became MikroTik Certified Network Associate <strong>(MTCNA),</strong> MikroTik Certified Routing Engineer <strong>(MTCRE)</strong> and MikroTik Certified User Management Engineer <strong>(MTCUME).</strong></p>
