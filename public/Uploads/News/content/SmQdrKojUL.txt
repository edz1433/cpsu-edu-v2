<p>CPSU- College of Business Management (CBM) nailed 2nd place in the cake decorating competition during the 2nd ASEAN-Inspired Hospitality and Tourism Skills Expo with the theme, &ldquo;ASEAN Matters: Epicentrum of Growth,&rdquo; on September 5, 2023 at Gaisano Grand Event Hall, Bacolod City.</p>

<p>Lea De Vicente, Rona Borbon, Fernando Jumawan and Johnrick Sagun, BSHM 4 students represented their College in the said competition, together with their coaches Ms. Charity A. Vergara CBM faculty, and Ms. Sheila A. Ignacio, CBM dean. The said event ran for three days, starting from September until September 6, 2023.</p>

<p><img alt="" src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/cbm expo 3 2023.jpg" class="img-responsive" /></p>

<p>Aside from this, the group also participated in other categories such as the ASEAN Booth Competition and Tour Guiding showcased by Rona Borbon and Nicky Jane Pladjero.</p>

<p>The ASEAN Exhibit and Skills Expo aimed to showcase the talents of HM students in the Province through various skills display that harness their potentials as pride of Negrenses for global competitiveness.</p>
