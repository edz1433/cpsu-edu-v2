<p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">October 6, 2023 – Central Philippines State University (CPSU) received a prestigious recognition from the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) National Office for making it to this year’s WURI and THE rankings along with other PH Universities.</span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">The said acknowledgment was given during the Internationalization Champions of Nations Building and Sustainability (ICONS) Awards 2023 held at the Philippine International Convention Center (PICC), Pasay City. It aimed to continuously celebrate the addition of new PHEIs recognized by international ranking bodies. As the highlight of the celebration, the event recognized the high-impact initiatives of the PHEIs towards sustainability, commendable performance in recognized international rankings and ratings, trailblazing efforts to advance internationalization, and valuable contributions to ASEAN’s regional prosperity.</span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><img alt="" src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/CHED-awards-Internationalization-Champions%20%200%202023.jpg" style="border-width: initial; border-color: initial; border-image: initial; height: 635.625px; width: 847.5px;"></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">Presenting the award were Hon. J. Prospero E. De Vera, III, Chair of the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), and Atty. Lily Freida Milla, CHED Director for International Affairs Service.</span></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><img alt="" src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/CHED-awards-Internationalization-Champions%20%201%202023.jpg" style="border-width: initial; border-color: initial; border-image: initial; height: 635.625px; width: 847.5px;"></p><p style="margin-right: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: &quot;Roboto Slab&quot;, serif;"><span style="font-family: Rubik;">Dr. Joean Mae G.
