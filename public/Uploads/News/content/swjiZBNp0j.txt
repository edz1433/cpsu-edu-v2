<p><strong>Central Philippines State University Main Campus</strong>&nbsp;has finally become one of the&nbsp;<strong>MikroTik Academies </strong>in the Philippines. Being part of the MikroTik Academy allows CPSU Main Campus to offer MikroTik Certified Network Associate (MTCNA) course for free to university students.</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Ryan B. Escorial, </strong><strong>the Dean </strong>of the College of Computer Studies, CPSU Main Campus attended the training and certifications exam sponsored by EVSU TC in partnership with Philippine Society of Information Technology Educators (PSITE-8) Inc. on July 30 &ndash; August 4, 2019 at Tanauan, Leyte, Philippines. The training and exam qualified Mr. Escorial to become an internaltional MikroTik Certified Academy Trainer. He also became MikroTik Certified Network Associate (MTCNA), MikroTik Certified Routing Engineer (MTCRE) and MikroTik Certified User Management Engineer (MTCUME).</p>
