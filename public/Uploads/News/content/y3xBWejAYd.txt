<p>Class 2021 graduates of Central Philippines State University accross campuses celebrated their successful academic accomplishment through a Baccalaureate Mass held last March 9, 2022 at the St. Francis Xavier Cathedral, Kabankalan City.</p>

<p>Amid the pandemic, the holy ceremony was conducted as an auspice of thanksgiving and a celebration of joy to honor the laudable achievement of the graduates and their families.</p>

<p>The Mass was celebrated by Most Reverend Bishop Louie Galbines, and co-celebrated by Rev. Fr. Romeo Tayco, Rev. Fr. Richard Lachica, Rev. Fr. Jose Severino Nacional III and Rev. Fr. Ritchie Villaflor.</p>

<p>This culmination event is a customary activity held by the University for all its graduands both in the undergraduate and graduate school (GS) programs. The formal graduation rites is set on March 31, 2022.</p>
