<p>A total of 9 Certificates of Program Compliance (COPCs) have been awarded to the CPSU-Graduate School by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) for its various academic programs offered in the Main Campus.&nbsp;</p>

<p><img alt="" src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/cpsu-grad-copc-2023.jpg" class="img-responsive" /></p>

<p>These programs were the Master of Arts in Education (MAED) majors in Mathematics, English, Filipino, Physical Education, Early Childhood Education, Social Science, General Science, Educational Management, and the Master in Public Administration (MPA). These COPCs were awarded last December 2022, with the latest for MPA released last January 30, 2023. These are as follows:</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>MAED-Mathematics - COPC # 094, series of 2022</p>

<p>MAED-English - COPC # 095, series of 2022</p>

<p>MAED-Filipino - COPC # 096, series of 2022</p>

<p>MAED-Physical Education - COPC # 097, series of 2022</p>

<p>MAED-Early Childhood Education - COPC # 098, series of 2022</p>

<p>MAED-Social Science - COPC # 099, series of 2022</p>

<p>MAED-General Science - COPC # 100, series of 2022</p>

<p>MAED-Educational Management &nbsp; - COPC # 101, series of 2022</p>

<p>Master in Public Administration&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; - COPC # 102, series of 2022</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>Dr. Riza Stephanie Abada-Alfaras, dean of the Graduate School, expressed genuine gratefulness to the taskforce, the administration and stakeholders for the support and assistance given towards this achievement.</p>

<p>Awarding of COPC signifies that the Commission recognizes the quality programs offered by the University Graduate School being fully compliant with its set policies, standards and guideline. This further ascertains the capability and competence of the Graduate school to operate the said programs.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Currently, the University is waiting for the COPC issuance for Ph.D. in Educational Management, while the Ph.D. in Agriculture, MS Agriculture and MS in Animal Science programs are awaiting for the validation of the technical panel committee.</p>
