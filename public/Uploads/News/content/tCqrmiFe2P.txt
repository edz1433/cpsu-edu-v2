<p>CPSU Main Campus - College of Teacher Education obtained 100% passing rate for its first takers in elementary level in the October 2022 Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Data from the Professional Regulation Commission released last December 16, 2022, showed that all its 17 first-time takers passed the said exam. The same data showed 14 out of 20 repeaters who also passed the exam for the elementary level.&nbsp;</p>

<p><img alt="" src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/COTED passers - PRC licensure exams-1.jpg" class="img-responsive" /></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><img alt="" src="https://cpsu.edu.ph/files/COTED passers - PRC licensure exams-2.jpg" class="img-responsive"/></p>

<p>Meanwhile, in the Secondary level, there were 77 out of the 102 first-time takers, and 18 repeaters who passed the exam.</p>

<p>Basing on these data, the elementary level obtained an overall performance of 83.78%, and the secondary level with 59.75%. These results exceeded the institutional target and the national passing percentage for both elementary and secondary levels.</p>
