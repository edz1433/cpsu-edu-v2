<p>Central Philippines State University (CPSU)- College of Teacher Education (COTED) lauds the 108 newly licensed Professional Teachers (LPT) for the March 2023 Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET) administered by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC).</p>

<p>The results were released on May 19, 2023 with a 64.93% overall performance rating for the Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSED). 80.81% (80 out of 99) of which arefirst-time takers and 20.00% repeaters.</p>

<p>Likewise, the Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED) garnered an overall performance rating of 77.27% where 85.71% (30 out of 35) are first-time takers, and 44.44% (4 out of 9) repeaters.</p>

<p>The COTED department, headed by Dean Merfe Cayot-Hucalinas, faculty and staff, and the whole CPSU community celebrate this remarkable endeavor with the successful examinees as they now join the pool of professional teachers in the country.</p>
