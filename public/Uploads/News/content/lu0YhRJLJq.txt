<p>CPSU- Victorias Campus has once again claimed victory after their second-in-a-row achievement in the Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET). Held last January 2022, the Campus garnered a sweet 100% passing rate in the Elementary Level, a victorious repeat of record for the said board exam as this extension is remembered to have achieved 100% passing rate plus one topnotcher in the September 2021 exam. The Campus Administrator, the teaching force of the College of Teacher Education, the staff, and the whole students are expressive with their heartfelt gratitude and congratulations to the passers for imprinting another valuable record to the institution.
