<p>The Central Philippines State University - College of Engineering (CPSU-COE) lauds the thirteen (13) New Registered Electrical Engineers (REEs) who took the licensure examination for Electrical Engineers conducted by the Professional Regulations Commission (PRC) last April 25-26, 2023.</p>

<p>With a 100% rating for the first takers (10/10 number of examinees), and 60% for the retakers (3/5 number of examinees), the College garnered an overall passing rate of 86.67%.</p>
